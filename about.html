About HomeShine Services

Professional cleaning services with 15+ years of experience, serving homes and apartments with dedication and care.

Our Story

Founded in 2009, HomeShine Services started with a simple mission: to provide reliable, high-quality cleaning services that homeowners can trust. What began as a small team has grown into one of the most trusted cleaning services in the New York area.

We've built our reputation on consistency, professionalism, and attention to detail. Every member of our team is carefully trained and vetted to ensure they meet our high standards.

Today, we serve hundreds of satisfied customers, providing everything from regular home cleaning to deep cleaning services.
